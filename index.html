Diabetes Report Generator v0.1.8
This tool allows users to upload files containing search results of diabetic patients and their latest lab values, and generates graphs based on the selected filters. 'Snapshot Mode' allows you to upload a single file which then creates a report that represents the performance of the practice for that point in time. It shows different diabetic measures and the percentage of patients that meet the target values. 'Tracking Mode' lets you upload multiple files at once, and allows you to visualize the performance of a particular diabetic measure over time, with one data point for each file uploaded. Filters allow you to select and deselect the physicians in your practice, to include or exclude them from your visualization. Images of the generated graphs can be saved by clicking on the "Save to PNG" button.
